#STREETMEETWARSAW Photo workshops powered by Puma

  • Client: Puma
  • Scope: Production

Streetmeet is a photography project that has been in development since 2019. Its main goal is to build a photographic community where participants engage and can share their experience, views, knowledge and passion in photography.

In 2020, the project takes place in the form of 2 photo workshops in Warsaw and Gdańsk (Streetmeetwarsaw, Streetmeet3city).

The ambassadors were six photographers from Poland, working with various techniques - this allowed us to expand the target group and reach out to more diverse, and creative people.

Project details

  • Cooperation with 6 photo ambassadors (Katarzyna Milewska, Paweł Fabjański, Dorota Szulc, Daniel Jaroszek, Łukasz Ziętek and Filip Blank).
  • Creating photo content with every ambassador and graphic content for communication.
  • Organization of a photo-workshops (2 events) - interesting locations, scenography, merch and various workshop topics.
  • PR support - communication with the media.
