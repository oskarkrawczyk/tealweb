BAKSFEST − digital festival INSTABAKS x PUMA

  • Client: Puma
  • Scope: Production

An event inspired by standard multi-day festivals, transferred to the online world - to one of the popular Facebook groups dedicated to topics related to street culture, fashion and shoes, INSTABAKS TALK! The founder and owner of the group is Kamil Tomaszewski, a well-known and respected shoe collector in our country.

In the era of a pandemic, when there is no possibility of participating in group events, consumers are looking for other, interesting events and activities.

The answer to this people's demand was BAKSFEST - a week under the patronage of PUMA, addressed to the users of the Facebook group. Every day, new attractions awaited the recipients, thanks to which they could not only receive entertainment and inspiration, but also better understand the image of the PUMA brand and its most important products.

The visual setting of the activation referred to music festivals. A dedicated merch and posters for the group were also created.

As part of activation

  • communication within the FB group and on the channels of the community creator.
  • support for influencers related to the subject of the group and being a real part of it.
  • customization of PUMA products; - substantive talks with representatives of various fields - football, motorsport, shoe collecting.
  • photo sessions & videos including products important to the PUMA brand.
