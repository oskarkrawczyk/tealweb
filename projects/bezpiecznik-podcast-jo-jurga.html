Social media strategy for podcast − #BEZPIECZNIK by Dr Jo Jurga

  • Client: Jo Jurga
  • Scope: Production

Dr Jo Jurga designs interiors dedicated to living in isolation. She specializes in the sense of security design, both on Earth and in outer space. Her research revolves around examining human senses and executing design solutions, especially for overstimulated users.

As part of her scientific activities, she also implements the #BEZPIECZNIK podcast, which is dedicated to the sense of security in the body, soul and space.

#BEZPIECZNIK is a podcast dedicated to the sense of security in the body, soul and space. Jo Jurga talks about the sensual perception of reality, neuroarchitecture and proxemics, or how we perceive the world around us and what we can do to make our life better.

Our goal was to support the author in the launch of the podcast and to develop a strategy for social media activities, which was to reach the audience through more lifestyle content, which was based on the scientific knowledge of Jo Jurga.

Our actions

  • Development of a full action strategy related to the launch of the #BEZPIECZNIK podcast and the author's subsequent activity in social media (mainly as part of the IG platform)
  • Creative concept and the implementation of an image photo session, which was used in the subsequent communication
  • PR support - positioning the author in the lifestyle & design media
