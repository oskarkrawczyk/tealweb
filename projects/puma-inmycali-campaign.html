Puma − #inmycali campaign

  • Client: Puma
  • Scope: 360°

The main goal for the PUMA #inmycali activation was to find creative young women and support their activities throughout the product.

We teamed up with polish lifestyle magazine Glamour. Together we chose 11 women (representing 9 different businesses) and published their stories in 3 articles supported by 3 dedicated photoshoots.

Additionally we organised an Instagram contest in which we looked for 3 creative girls who would join PUMA #inmycali squad and are their stories on Glamour.pl.

The project consists of 3 stages

  • Creative shipment
  • PUMA #inmycali x Glamour - photoshoots and interviews with selected creative girls
  • Instagram contest

Project details
