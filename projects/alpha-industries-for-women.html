Alpha Industries for Women

  • Client: Alpha Industries
  • Scope: Production

The Alpha Industries brand with military roots, known primarily in the men's world, this time decided to direct its activities to women. In order to engage female recipients, we have prepared a personalized creative mailing embedded in botanical, green aesthetics and photo sessions with selected influencers.
