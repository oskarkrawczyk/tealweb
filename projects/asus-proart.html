ASUS "Let the pros speak" campaign

  • Client: ASUS
  • Scope: Production

We had the opportunity to create a creative concept and then implement it. It assumed the involvement of 2 artists from the creative industry, as well as the agency itself and people working on the campaign, to do their work using Asus laptops and immortalize the effects in the form of video materials.

We presented the campaigns from the backstage, which gave a genuine insight into the functionality of the equipment.

We allowed observers to backstage the work of many people involved in the project - an agency, camera operators, retouchers, video editor, music producer and artists.

One computer - Asus ProArt Studiobook 16 OLED. Lots of tests, glances and ways to use it.

Project details

  • Involvement of 2 artists.
  • Creating 8 video materials presenting different views on the possibilities of the equipment - backstage.
  • Project communication via social media channels of the people involved - brand, artists and agency.
  • Round table − a longer, technical video recording the feelings of the people involved in the project.
