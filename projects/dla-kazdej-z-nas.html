"Dla Każdej z Nas" FB group dedicated to women under the patronage of RESERVED

  • Client: Reserved
  • Scope: Production

We used a non-standard format to create a Facebook group under the patronage of the Reserved brand. A space dedicated to women, established to the mutual dose of inspiration, experiences, views and everything that is important for the group members. After the first month of operation, the group already numbered 3.5 thousand members, since then their number has been growing steadily. Growth is organic.
