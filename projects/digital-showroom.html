Digital Showroom

  • Client: teal.
  • Scope: Production

Showroom.digital is an innovative tool and the first fully virtual showroom in Poland, which aims to improve work between brands from the fashion & lifestyle sector, stylists and journalists. It is also a response to the changing conditions of the entire fashion world in which a large part of movements and events is transferred to the virtual sphere. Most of the showroom.digital activities, apart from shipping and pickup, take place online.

Overriding goals - streamlining work, convenience, time saving, and 24/7 availability.
  • Brand products are displayed on the platform in a variety of ways. Their search is carried out by browsing, filtering or manually searching for the item we are interested in.
  • Recipients can register via the main site, then their accounts are verified. As part of them, you can order clothes, shoes and accessories from brands that have their showroom inside the platform.

The form of loaning products for presentation in creative projects and in the media is nothing new. Until now, however, it was based on the traditional scheme of visiting spaces located in various parts of the city, which often required a lot of time and energy to carry bags, which, along with visiting subsequent locations, were filled with a large number of various things.

The virtual showroom was to change this pattern of operation.
